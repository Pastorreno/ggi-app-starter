# Websites in the GGI Solutions Ecosystem

## Core Decision

Websites should remain part of the GGI Solutions product ecosystem, but they should not be the main niche.

The main niche is not "web design."

The stronger positioning is:

```text
GGI Solutions builds digital systems that help organizations communicate, capture people, follow up, and grow.
```

Websites are one of the front doors into the system.

---

## Strategic Role of Websites

Websites should be positioned as:

```text
Digital front doors
```

A website is where people land, learn, register, inquire, book, or take the next step.

But the real value comes from what happens after they click:

- lead capture
- registration
- follow-up
- messaging world invite
- onboarding
- growth path
- dashboard/reporting

---

## What GGI Should Not Sell

Do not lead with:

```text
I build websites.
```

That is too generic and too crowded.

Instead say:

```text
We build digital front doors and follow-up systems that turn attention into action.
```

## Final Recommendation

Websites should be a product line, not the identity of the company.

GGI Solutions should not be known as a web design company.

GGI Solutions should be known as a systems company that builds websites when a website is the right front door.

Final sentence:

```text
GGI Solutions builds websites as digital front doors that connect people to follow-up, messaging, onboarding, and development systems.
```
