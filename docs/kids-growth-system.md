# Kids Growth System

## Strategic Role

GGI Solutions also has a dedicated system for kids and youth.

This should not be treated as a smaller version of the adult system. Children and youth need language, structure, assessments, reflection, and support pathways designed specifically for their stage of development.

The kids system sits inside the larger PurposePath ecosystem.

---

## Product Relationship

```text
PurposePath OS = identity, why, wiring, and growth discovery
GrowthPath OS = ongoing development, support, progress, and accountability
LeaderRise OS = future leadership, service, responsibility, and multiplication
Kids Growth System = age-appropriate identity, character, confidence, and purpose development for children and youth
```

## Recommended Product Name Options

## Core Framework

## 1. Know Me

Helping children understand identity, strengths, feelings, interests, and personality.

Question:

```text
Who am I?
```

## 2. Grow Me

Helping children build character, habits, responsibility, confidence, and emotional awareness.

Question:

```text
How can I grow?
```

## 3. Guide Me

Helping adults, mentors, teachers, and parents know how to support the child.

Question:

```text
What support do I need?
```

## 4. Show Me

Helping children practice growth through activities, service, creativity, leadership moments, and positive responsibility.

Question:

```text
What can I try next?
```

## 5. Celebrate Me

Helping adults affirm progress, milestones, effort, and growth.

Question:

```text
What progress should be celebrated?
```

## Product Ladder

## BrightPath Kids Starter

For small programs that need a basic child/youth growth tracker.

Includes:

- child/youth intake
- strengths and interests profile
- basic character/growth categories
- parent/mentor notes
- simple progress tracker

## BrightPath Kids Growth

For schools, churches, youth programs, or childcare centers that need structured support.

Includes:

- everything in Starter
- growth pathways
- mentor/teacher/parent support notes
- behavior and character reflection
- progress dashboard
- monthly review rhythm

## BrightPath Kids Pro

For organizations that need a full child/youth development operating system.

Includes:

- everything in Growth
- custom age bands
- group dashboards
- parent communication templates
- activity library
- progress reports
- staff training
- implementation support

---

## Age Bands

Recommended structure:

```text
Early Childhood: ages 3-5
Elementary: ages 6-10
Middle School: ages 11-13
High School: ages 14-18
```

Each age band needs different language and activities.

---

## Guardrails

BrightPath Kids should not be positioned as therapy, diagnosis, clinical treatment, or mental health care.

It should be positioned as:

- growth support
- character development
- reflection
- mentoring
- confidence building
- parent/teacher visibility
- development tracking
- positive next steps

Adult review and consent should be required when used with minors.
