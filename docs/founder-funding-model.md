# Founder Funding Model for GGI Solutions

## Core Conviction

GGI Solutions is not being built for mass profit, corporate greed, or status.

The company is being built to:

1. Take care of Reno's wife and household responsibly.
2. Fund ministry ideas and kingdom work.
3. Create resources for tent ministry, travel ministry, and ministry team support.
4. Use AI employees and digital systems to increase capacity without replacing human authenticity.

## Financial Purpose

The goal is provision and mission fuel.

```text
Provision first. Ministry fuel second. Greed never.
```

## Ministry Vision Being Funded

GGI Solutions should help create resources for:

- buying a ministry tent
- traveling for ministry
- paying a ministry team to travel
- supporting music/worship/media/team members
- launching people-development events
- funding wild ministry ideas with structure
- building systems that help people walk in purpose

## Business Philosophy

Money is a tool, not the master.

The company should be profitable enough to be sustainable, generous, and mission-capable.

It should not chase profit in a way that compromises people, values, or purpose.

## AI Employees Principle

AI employees should be used to increase capacity, reduce admin load, and help Reno execute.

They should not replace human care, ministry authenticity, or real relationships.

AI helps carry operations so people can carry mission.

## Public Language Guardrail

Do not publicly market GGI Solutions as a company trying to maximize profit.

Use language like:

```text
We build systems that help organizations serve people better.
```

```text
We use modern tools to create clearer pathways, stronger communication, and better follow-up.
```

Internally, the financial mission is:

```text
Build a sustainable company that provides for family and funds ministry.
```

## Offer Strategy

The company should keep practical cash-flow offers:

- basic websites
- event landing pages
- messaging worlds
- custom qualifiers
- follow-up systems

These create income quickly and help fund larger mission work.

## Guardrails

GGI Solutions should not:

- exploit clients
- manipulate people with fear
- overcharge without value
- chase vanity scale
- automate away humanity
- prioritize money over mission

GGI Solutions should:

- price fairly
- deliver excellently
- communicate clearly
- follow up consistently
- serve with integrity
- use profit to support household and ministry purpose

## Final Statement

GGI Solutions exists to build sustainable income, support family responsibility, and fund ministry work that helps people walk in purpose.

The company is not greed-driven.

It is provision-driven and mission-fueled.
