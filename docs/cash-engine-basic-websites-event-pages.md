# GGI Solutions Cash Engine: Basic Websites + Event Landing Pages

## Decision

GGI Solutions should intentionally offer basic websites and event landing pages as quick-turnaround cash-flow services.

These offers are not the full long-term vision, but they are valuable entry products that can bring in revenue quickly, build trust, and create opportunities for larger systems work.

---

## Why This Matters

People are already asking Reno for websites.

That means there is current market demand.

GGI Solutions should not ignore demand.

The strategy is:

```text
Sell quick-turnaround websites and event pages now.
Use those projects to build relationships.
Upsell into smart qualifiers, follow-up systems, messaging worlds, and development systems later.
```
