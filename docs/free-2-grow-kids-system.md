# Free 2 Grow Kids System

## Core Clarification

Free 2 Grow is the child-focused product line inside the larger GGI Solutions development ecosystem.

It should not be treated as a generic kids feature, and it should not be buried under adult development language.

Free 2 Grow is for kids.

## Free 2 Grow Framework

## 1. Know Me

Helping kids understand their identity, strengths, feelings, interests, and personality.

Question:

```text
Who am I?
```

## 2. Grow Me

Helping kids build character, confidence, habits, responsibility, and emotional awareness.

Question:

```text
How can I grow?
```

## 3. Guide Me

Helping adults understand how to support the child.

Question:

```text
What support do I need?
```

## 4. Show Me

Helping kids practice growth through activities, creativity, service, responsibility, and positive action.

Question:

```text
What can I try next?
```

## 5. Celebrate Me

Helping adults celebrate effort, progress, milestones, and growth.

Question:

```text
What progress should be celebrated?
```

## Product Ladder

## Free 2 Grow Starter

For small programs that need basic child/youth tracking.

Includes:

- child/youth intake
- strengths and interests profile
- character/growth categories
- parent/mentor notes
- simple progress tracker

## Free 2 Grow Growth

For churches, schools, childcare centers, and youth programs that need structured support.

Includes:

- everything in Starter
- growth pathways
- adult support notes
- behavior and character reflection
- progress dashboard
- monthly review rhythm

## Free 2 Grow Pro

For organizations that need a full child/youth development operating system.

Includes:

- everything in Growth
- custom age bands
- group dashboards
- parent communication templates
- activity library
- progress reports
- staff training
- implementation support

## Guardrails

Free 2 Grow should not be positioned as therapy, diagnosis, or clinical treatment.

It should be positioned as:

- growth support
- character development
- confidence building
- reflection
- mentoring
- parent/teacher visibility
- development tracking
- positive next steps

Adult review and consent should be required when used with minors.
