# AI Video Plan for GGI Solutions

## Decision

GGI Solutions can use AI-generated video assets instead of requiring Reno to appear on camera immediately.

The website should support:

- founder-style welcome video
- product explainer video
- service demo videos
- short clips embedded from YouTube

## Recommended First Videos

## 1. Welcome to the Hub

Purpose:

Create trust and help visitors understand the heart of GGI Solutions.

Length:

```text
30-45 seconds
```

Message:

```text
Welcome to the Hub. GGI Solutions helps organizations build simple digital systems that move people forward. Whether you need a website, event page, messaging world, qualifier, or follow-up system, we help you choose the right starting point and build from there.
```

## 2. What We Build

Purpose:

Explain the connected system visually.

Length:

```text
45-60 seconds
```

Message:

```text
A website captures attention. A qualifier finds the need. A messaging world keeps people connected. A follow-up system makes sure nobody gets missed.
```

## 3. Why It Matters

Purpose:

Communicate the caring people-first heart behind the company.

Length:

```text
30-45 seconds
```

Message:

```text
People should not feel lost after they reach out. GGI Solutions builds systems that help organizations guide people clearly, follow up consistently, and support the right next step.
```

## AI Video Style

Use a professional, modern, Apple-inspired but warm visual tone.

Visual direction:

- dark premium background
- blue and purple light accents
- clean text overlays
- abstract system-flow animations
- people-focused imagery without looking stocky
- no fake talking head unless high quality
- calm but inspiring music

## Recommended Tools

Use any of these depending on access:

- Google Veo / Gemini video tools if available
- Runway
- Pika
- Canva video
- CapCut templates
- Descript
- HeyGen or Synthesia for avatar only if it looks professional

## Best First Approach

Start with non-avatar AI motion videos.

Reason:

AI avatar talking heads can look fake and reduce trust if quality is low.

Better first version:

- voiceover
- animated text
- abstract motion
- product mockup footage
- screenshots of the website
- calm music

## Voiceover Options

Option 1:

Use Reno's real voice later.

Option 2:

Use professional AI voiceover for first version.

Option 3:

Use text-only video with music if voiceover is not ready.

## YouTube Workflow

1. Generate video.
2. Export as MP4.
3. Upload to YouTube as unlisted or public.
4. Copy YouTube embed ID.
5. Replace placeholder embed links in the homepage.
