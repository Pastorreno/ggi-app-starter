# Website Inquiries Service Package

## Why This Exists

People are asking GGI Solutions to build websites. That means websites should become a packaged entry offer, but they should not become the whole company identity.

GGI Solutions should sell websites as digital front doors that connect into lead capture, follow-up, messaging, onboarding, and growth systems.

---

## Core Positioning

Do not say:

```text
I build websites.
```

Say:

```text
I build clean websites that help people take the next step — inquire, book, register, join, or connect.
```

Better:

```text
GGI Solutions builds digital front doors: websites, landing pages, and simple systems that turn attention into action.
```

---

## Website Offer Name

Recommended name:

```text
LaunchSite Systems
```

Simple explanation:

```text
A LaunchSite is a clean website or landing page built to help people understand your offer and take action.
```

---

## Website Packages

## 1. Starter LaunchSite

Best for individuals, small businesses, ministries, coaches, and simple offers.

Includes:

- one-page website
- mobile-friendly layout
- about/offer section
- call-to-action section
- contact button or form
- basic SEO title/description

Price:

```text
$500-$1,000
```

---

## 2. Growth LaunchSite

Best for businesses, nonprofits, ministries, and organizations that need more structure.

Includes:

- up to 4 pages
- homepage
- about page
- services/programs page
- contact/inquiry page
- lead capture form
- basic follow-up tracker recommendation

Price:

```text
$1,500-$3,500
```

---

## 3. System LaunchSite

Best for organizations that need a website connected to a real workflow.

Includes:

- website or funnel pages
- inquiry/registration/intake form
- follow-up workflow
- WhatsApp/Telegram community link if needed
- simple dashboard or tracking sheet
- launch support

Price:

```text
$3,500-$7,500+
```

## Response Script for Website Inquiries

```text
Absolutely, I can help with that. I build clean websites and landing pages through GGI Solutions, but I usually build them with the next step in mind — not just a page that looks good.

The goal is to help people understand who you are, what you offer, and what action to take next, whether that is booking, registering, calling, joining, or submitting an inquiry.

I have a few options depending on what you need: a simple one-page LaunchSite, a more complete multi-page site, or a site connected to forms, follow-up, WhatsApp/Telegram, or a tracking system.

The first step is for me to learn what the site is for and what you want visitors to do once they land on it.
```

Registration form

GGI Solutions should build websites that create action.
